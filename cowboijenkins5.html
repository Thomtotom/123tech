About us

Hello and welcome to our website!

This website is an activity index, which hopes to help people learn many things.
Te Reo Maori is one of the courses you can choose from. There are lots of educational
games on a range of subjects. We are from Wellington, New Zealand.

We are competing in a competition called 123tech. 123tech is about young coders and
problem solvers having the opportunity to make an app or a website. If you were to join,
you would contribute to solving a problem in your community by using digital technology.

We hope you enjoy it!
