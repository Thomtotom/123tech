Stuck in the mud

10 or more people

1. Choose someone to be tagger. They count to 10 while evryone else runs away.
2. When they tag someone, that person must stick their arms out. They are "Stuck in the mud".
3. When someone is stuck in the mud, two people can help by holding their arms.a person holding someone's arm cannot be tagged. They are now free.
4. Repeat forever or until less than 2 people are not tagged.
