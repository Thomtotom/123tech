Draw a dragon!

1. First, draw a shape like a backwards z, then add a line as shown.

2. In the upper bit, behind the middle of the z, draw an egg shape then an oval inside to make an eye.

3. Draw a curved line as shown, then another line next to that. Then, extend both and curve slightly, and add a line between the ends of the first two.

4. Draw a horn shape opposite to the other one, with a line in the middle.

5. Add Teeth.

6. Draw a curved line under the teeth, then extend into a neck.

7. Extend from the neck with a fatter body, then thin and curve into a tail.

8. Add one wing, with a curved front and several short curves on the back.

9. Add a line along your first wing, then draw another wing a bit further forward.

10. Add a line on the edge of your second wing.

11. Add the first leg with two v-shapes then a three-pointed claw.

12. Add the second leg behind the first.

13. The end. Now colour and add decorations!
