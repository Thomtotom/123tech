Welcome to the riddle page!

I am the beginning of everything, the end of time and space. The beginning of eternity, and the end of every place. What am I?

I must eat to survive, but if I drink, I will die. What am I?

I need light to survive, but will die if light touches me. What am I?

I am the middle of the middle and the end of the end. What am I?

What walks on four legs in the morning, two legs at noon, and three legs in the evening?

What comes twice in a moment, once in a minute, but never in a thousand years?

The more of me you take, the more you leave behind. What am I?

Poor people have me, rich people need me, and if you eat me, you die. What am I?

What do we want most of all, but do not know that we have it when we get it?

What runs without legs?

I am always coming, but never reaching you. What am I?

What has cities, but no houses; forests, but no trees; and water, but no fish?

What has people but no houses, trees but no forests, water but no lakes, and night but no day?

What starts with a T, ends with a T, and has T in it?

What has to be taken before it can be given?

What has a head and a tail, but no body?

I am as light as a feather, yet no one can hold me for more than a few minutes. What am I?

The more you take away, the more I become. What am I?

What goes up but never comes down?

If you have me, you may want to share me. If you share me, you lose me. What am I?
