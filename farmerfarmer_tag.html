Farmer Farmer

5 or more people

1. Choose two boundaries that the players have to run across, and two that they can't go outside.
2. Choose someone to be in.
3. Everyone who is not in calls out "Farmer, Farmer, may we cross your golden river?" The person who is in calls a certain aspect, for example "only if you're wearing green!" Everyone who has that aspect is safe, and everyone else can be tagged.
4. If you are tagged, you are a tagger.
5. Once everyone is tagged, the game is over. Alternatively, the last person not tagged can be in for the next round.
