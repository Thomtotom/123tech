Plant a garden

Requires:

Container (ice cream container or plant pot)
Soil/potting mix
Saucer
Seed packets (from a garden center)

If your container doesn’t have holes, make some using scissors. Be careful not to hurt yourself.
Place the container on the saucer, and add soil. If it feels dry, add a little water.
Follow planting instructions on the seed packet.
Leave in a sheltered place outside or a sunny window.
Check daily, watering if necessary.
Note: herbs are recommended for quick growth and usefulness, but other plants can work.
