Cheese Puffs

These are super easy to make and delicious! We like jam on ours…

Ingredients

1 C flour
3 tsp baking powder
2 C grated cheese
1 egg, beaten
1/2 C milk

METHOD

1. Preheat oven to 200°C
2. Mix all the ingredients together in a large bowl and place spoonfuls in greased patty tins or on a tray.
3. Bake for 12-15 minutes.
4. Makes 3 large or 10 small.
