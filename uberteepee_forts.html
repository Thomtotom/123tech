Uberteepee

Materials

Duvet/blanket/sheet
Seven poles, roughly equal length
Several clothespegs
2 elastic ties

1. Tie three poles together at the top, then repeat.
2. Tie one pole between the two bunches of 3.
3. Stand the whole thing up on four of the poles, with the fifth in the middle.
4. Drape the balnket over the whole thing.
5. Use clothespegs to hold blanket on.
