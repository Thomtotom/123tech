Go home Stay home

3 or more people

1. Choose a base, where everyone will be trying to get to.
2. Choose an area which the tagger can stay inside. This is not always necessary, but will make the game easier.
3. Choose a tagger. They will guard the base.
4. The tagger counts to 20 while everyone hides.
5. Everyone tries to get to the base. If they are tagged, they are out, and if they make it, they have won.
