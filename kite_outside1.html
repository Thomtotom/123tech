Make a kite!

Requires:

Tape
Paper
String
Ribbon
Skewers

1. Tape two skewers together to get a longer skewer.

2. Tape another skewer on across from that.

3. Take a piece of paper and place the shape on it. Then, trace around it with the pencil.

4. Take the skewers and tape off the paper, then cut the shape out. Then put the shape back on the paper. Tape the corners on.

5. Cut off 30 cm of string and tie on to the back. Cut the ribbon into 10cm pieces and tie onto the string.

6. Now, go fly a kite!
